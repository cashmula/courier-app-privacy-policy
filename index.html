Privacy Policy — Courier (Business, Driver, and Customer apps)

Effective date: July 19, 2026

This policy covers three related apps published by hafmula-group: Courier — Business, Courier — Driver, and Courier — Track Your Delivery (together, "the Apps"). They share one backend, so this single policy covers all three.

Who we are

The Apps are operated by hafmula-group. For any question about this policy or your data, contact hafmula-group@yahoo.com.

Data we collect

Account information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and password. Passwords are never stored in plain text — our authentication provider (Supabase) stores only a securely hashed form.

Profile information, depending on your role:

  • Business accounts: business name, business address, phone number.
  • Courier accounts: full name, phone number, vehicle type, and an availability status (available / busy / offline) used internally to route jobs.
  • Customer accounts: full name, phone number.

Merchant information, for business accounts that opt in to being listed: category, description, a photo, and an approximate map location, plus the items on their menu (name, description, price, photo).

Order information. Pickup address, drop-off address, optional package notes, items ordered (for orders placed directly with a listed merchant), and the order's status history (e.g. pending → assigned → picked up → delivered), timestamped automatically as it changes.

Ratings and reviews. Once an order is delivered, the customer may rate the business and the courier (1-5 stars, optional comment). A rating is tied to the specific order and the two people involved in it.

Push notification tokens. A device identifier used to deliver notifications about order updates (new job available, order accepted, status changes) — not used for anything besides sending these notifications, and you can deny notification permission at any time.

Location information. The Driver app records the courier's live GPS coordinates every few seconds whenever the courier has toggled themselves "online" (available for jobs) — not only during an active delivery — so new orders can be offered to nearby couriers first. We store only the current position, never a location history, and it is overwritten on each update. Location sharing stops the moment a courier toggles themselves offline or signs out. The Business and Track Delivery apps may also ask for your device's approximate location, used only to sort/display nearby listed merchants — it is not stored or sent to us.

We do not use advertising or analytics SDKs of any kind — no ad networks, no analytics trackers, no crash-reporting services. The only data we collect is what's listed above, collected directly through your use of the Apps.

How we use your data

  • To create and secure your account.
  • To create, assign, and fulfill delivery orders between businesses, couriers, and customers, including orders customers place directly with a listed merchant.
  • To show live delivery tracking to the business and customer for an order in progress, and to offer new orders to nearby available couriers first.
  • To maintain a status history for each order (for support and dispute resolution).
  • To send push notifications about your own orders/jobs (new job available, order accepted, status changes).
  • To display ratings so businesses, couriers, and customers can see feedback from past orders.

Who can see your data

Access is restricted by role at the database level (not just in the app UI):

  • A business can only see its own orders.
  • A courier can only see orders assigned to them.
  • A customer can only see their own orders.
  • Currently, businesses and customers do not see a courier's name or phone number inside the Apps — only the order status and, once a courier is assigned, that courier's live location for tracking purposes.
  • A courier's live location can only be written by that courier's own authenticated device — no one else can submit a position on their behalf.
  • A listed business's menu (category, description, photo, items, prices) is visible to anyone using the Apps, the same way it would be on any public storefront.
  • Ratings (star rating and any comment) are visible to any signed-in user of the Apps, not just the two people who were part of that order — this lets customers see a merchant's or courier's reputation before ordering.

Where your data is stored

We use two service providers to store data on our behalf, and do not sell or share your data with anyone else:

  • Supabase — stores account credentials, profile information, merchant/order/rating data, and push notification device tokens (PostgreSQL database with row-level security).
  • Firebase (Google) — Realtime Database stores only a courier's current live position while they're online; Cloud Messaging delivers push notifications to your device using the token described above.

Data retention and deletion

We retain account and order data for as long as your account is active. The Apps do not currently have a self-service "delete my account" option. To request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data, email hafmula-group@yahoo.com and we will act on your request.

Children's privacy

The Apps are not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date above.
