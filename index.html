Privacy Policy — Courier (Business, Driver, and Customer apps)

Effective date: July 18, 2026

This policy covers three related apps published by hafmula-group: Courier — Business, Courier — Driver, and Courier — Track Your Delivery (together, "the Apps"). They share one backend, so this single policy covers all three.

Who we are

The Apps are operated by hafmula-group. For any question about this policy or your data, contact hafmula-group@yahoo.com.

Data we collect

Account information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and password. Passwords are never stored in plain text — our authentication provider (Supabase) stores only a securely hashed form.

Profile information, depending on your role:

  • Business accounts: business name, business address, phone number.
  • Courier accounts: full name, phone number, vehicle type, and an availability status (available / busy / offline) used internally to route jobs.
  • Customer accounts: full name, phone number.

Order information. Pickup address, drop-off address, optional package notes, and the order's status history (e.g. pending → assigned → picked up → delivered), timestamped automatically as it changes.

Location information. While a courier has an active delivery, the Driver app records the courier's live GPS coordinates every few seconds so the order can be tracked. We store only the current position — not a location history — and it is overwritten on each update. Location is only collected while the Driver app is actively broadcasting for a delivery in progress; it is not collected in the background otherwise.

We do not use advertising or analytics SDKs of any kind — no ad networks, no analytics trackers, no crash-reporting services. The only data we collect is what's listed above, collected directly through your use of the Apps.

How we use your data

  • To create and secure your account.
  • To create, assign, and fulfill delivery orders between businesses, couriers, and customers.
  • To show live delivery tracking to the business and customer for an order in progress.
  • To maintain a status history for each order (for support and dispute resolution).

Who can see your data

Access is restricted by role at the database level (not just in the app UI):

  • A business can only see its own orders.
  • A courier can only see orders assigned to them.
  • A customer can only see their own orders.
  • Currently, businesses and customers do not see a courier's name or phone number inside the Apps — only the order status and, once a courier is assigned, that courier's live location for tracking purposes.
  • A courier's live location can only be written by that courier's own authenticated device — no one else can submit a position on their behalf. It can only be viewed by the business or customer who actually has an active order with that courier.

Where your data is stored

We use two service providers to store data on our behalf, and do not sell or share your data with anyone else:

  • Supabase — stores account credentials, profile information, and order data (PostgreSQL database with row-level security).
  • Firebase (Google) Realtime Database — stores only a courier's current live position while a delivery is active.

Data retention and deletion

We retain account and order data for as long as your account is active. The Apps do not currently have a self-service "delete my account" option. To request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data, email hafmula-group@yahoo.com and we will act on your request.

Children's privacy

The Apps are not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date above.
